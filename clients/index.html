The Companies We Keep.

Like Associations International itself, the associations we partner with are a diverse group with plenty of shared interests: a common vision, a collective ambition, and a passion to improve the world through the services and products they offer.

Accounting and Financial Women’s Alliance & Foundation (AFWA/F)

The Accounting & Financial Women’s Alliance is a network of successful, motivated, and influential professionals that serves women in all accounting and finance fields by helping them achieve their full potential and contribute more to their profession.

Alliance for Women in Media & Foundation (AWM/F)

The Alliance for Women in Media and its Foundation supports educational programs and provides scholarships to deserving, up-and-coming women in media each year. The Foundation also produces the nationally acclaimed Gracie Awards, which honor exemplary programming created by, for, and about women.

American Volleyball Coaches Association (AVCA)

AVCA is the largest organization of volleyball coaches in the world, with over 7,000 members. Their mission is to advance the sport of volleyball with AVCA coaches at the center of leadership, advocacy, and professional development.

Association of Professional Dog Trainers (APDT/F)

The Association of Professional Dog Trainers is an organization of 5,500 trainers who are committed to excellence through education. APDT joined AI in January of 2015.

International Coaching Federation & Foundation (ICF/F)

ICF is the largest resource for business and personal coaches, representing more than 30,000 members and 25,000 credential holders. ICF supports and preserves the integrity of coaching through standards supported by the individual membership.

International SPA Association & Foundation (ISPA/F)

ISPA is recognized worldwide as the leading professional organization and voice of the spa industry. Founded in 1991, ISPA advances the spa industry by providing valuable educational and networking opportunities, promoting the value of the spa experience, and speaking as the authoritative voice to foster industry growth.

Kentucky Petroleum Marketers Association (KPMA)

KPMA is a state association representing 175 associate members who own, supply or service more than 2,300 retail fueling facilities in Kentucky. KPMA keeps its members up to date on issues and trends that affect the petroleum marketing industry.

National Association of Shell Marketers (NASM)

NASM provides its marketer members with guidance to enhance their Shell-branded business by acting as a conduit to Shell personnel and programs, providing access to fellow marketers for timely knowledge and sharing best practices.  

Quest Oracle Community (Quest)

Quest Oracle Community is the home to more than 65,000 users of Oracle’s JD Edwards, PeopleSoft, Cloud applications, and Database products. Quest connects Oracle users to technology leaders, Oracle experts and fellow users from companies who are driving innovation and leading through their use of Oracle products.
