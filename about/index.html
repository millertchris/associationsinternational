Taking Our Clients to the Next Level.

Associations International is a full-service association management firm providing services and support for international and domestic associations. Our large employee base and short client list means we can focus on the needs of each of our partner associations, helping them build their memberships, promote their missions, and support their value. Above all, we nourish their ability to grow.

Standing Out in the Crowd.

Capabilities

Associations International is an industry leader accredited by the AMC Institute, the global trade association representing the Associations Management Industry. We manage associations with membership ranging from 125 to over 50,000. 

Resources

Each association gets a dedicated staff assigned to meet its needs. The staff will have intimate knowledge of the association and its mission. Other services — like finance, human resources, technology, and member services — are shared among associations.

Culture

We aren’t the only ones who think of ourselves as family. Our clients agree. Like family, we’re flexible when times are tough, focused when times are challenging. But more important, like family, we’ve always got each others’ backs.
