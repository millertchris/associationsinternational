Sarah S. Pitt

Vice President Human Resources, Associations International (AI)

sarah.pitt@theaiedge.com

Bio

Sarah is a Lexington, Kentucky native and has over 20+ years of progressive experience as a Human Resources leader. She has held senior leadership positions in a national governmental non-profit and association management firm, healthcare and corporate management companies, and in higher education the University of Kentucky and Eastern Kentucky University. She also served as faculty at EKU. Sarah has been passionate about helping others since early on, majoring in Social Work and Psychology, earning her Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees from the University of Kentucky. She also holds a Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) certification and has been active in the Bluegrass Society for Human Resource Management and the national Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM), holding board positions. She also holds certifications in leadership and holistic coaching and has collaborated on leadership publications.
