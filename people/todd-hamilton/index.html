Todd Hamilton

Assistant Executive Director & COO, International Coach Federation (ICF)

todd.hamilton@coachfederation.org

Bio

Todd has been with Associations International for 21 years. Currently he serves as the Assistant Executive Director of the International Coach Federation (ICF). Previously Todd served as Executive Director of International Inbound Travel Association, Assistant Executive Director of the American Volleyball Coaches Association and Assistant Director of Industry, Government Affairs and Governance for the National Tour Association. Todd has served on numerous for-profit and non-profit boards of directors. He attended the University of Pikeville and University of Kentucky, with degrees in political science.
